# Meeting Memo

**Date:** 12 November 2025
**Participants:**  Dr. Jumpol Polvichai, Cyprien Diederichs


## Summary

During the meeting held on **12 November** with **Dr. Jumpol Polvichai & Cyprien Diederichs**, the following actions were agreed upon: the participants expressed strong enthusiasm for the project and agreed that the overall concept is highly promising.

However, a major challenge was identified regarding the data collection process.

## Key Discussion Points

- The participants appreciated the proposed approach and considered it to have significant potential.
- The main limitation is that most relevant web content is available in **Thai** and **English**.
- At present, there is no sufficiently reliable machine learning solution for accurately processing Thai-language content at the required level of quality and consistency.
- This language barrier is expected to have a significant impact on automated web data collection and analysis and should be considered when defining the project's scope and methodology.

## Conclusion

The participants agreed that the project is well designed and has strong potential. However, in its current form, it is **not considered viable for deployment in Thailand** due to the limitations of existing machine learning technologies for processing Thai-language content. These language-related constraints would significantly affect the reliability and effectiveness of the proposed solution.
